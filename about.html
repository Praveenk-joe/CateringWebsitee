From The Desk of Mala Mami

I, Mrs.Mala, The Managing Director of MalaMurthy Catering, was called as Murukku mami, some 20 yrs ago... We started our career with seer bakshanam in the early 1990.... Then I joined my dad, Late.Shri.Rajamani Iyer, who was a cook and cooks for all occasions and later started Mala Murthy catering, Starting from carrier meals to people working in offices, banks and to smaller it firms.. Later we started for the events in the year 1996 and did first wedding contract at sivanandha memorial hall in Coimbatore.

Now I proudly say We MalaMurthy Catering is one among the thought of mind brand for all coimbatorians, exclusively for catering service. We have organised more than 100 weddings, annadhanams in famous temples and all events at our guest home.... We create memories to our guest by our mouth watering delicious dishes. We provide the best menu as per the event and budget, marked by the client and a perfect cost-effective catering services. We offer different types of dishes like South Indian and North Indian and Chinese varieties. We feel that it is our responsibility to make an event more memorable with our delightful dishes food. We have best team of experts who provide comprehensive catering services. Our talented chefs, work with the finest seasonal and sustainable ingredients to create five-star level food that will be exactly right for your occasion.

And now MalaMurthy Catering is not only a catering organiser, but also an Event Management company... We organise entire range of services needed for a guest(whatever the event be) at a single roof...
